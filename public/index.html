Rules — OmeDash

Purpose
These rules exist to keep OmeDash safe, respectful, and enjoyable for everyone. By using the app you agree to follow them.

Who may use
• You must be at least 18 years old to use OmeDash.
• Minors (under 18) are strictly prohibited. Accounts found to belong to minors will be removed.

Basic conduct
• Treat other users with respect. Harassment, hate speech, threats, bullying, or targeted abuse are prohibited.
• Obtain explicit consent before sharing sexual content or initiating sexually explicit conversations. If consent is not given, stop immediately.
• Do not impersonate other people, public figures, or company representatives.

Prohibited content and behavior
Do not do any of the following while using OmeDash:
1. Share, request, or exchange sexual content involving minors or content that sexualizes minors.
2. Post or send illegal content (drugs, weapons, stolen goods, or explicit instructions for illegal acts).
3. Share private personal data (full name, home address, ID numbers, social security numbers, private photos, bank details) of yourself or others. Doxxing is prohibited.
4. Engage in or promote hate, racism, xenophobia, or discrimination against protected groups.
5. Threaten, stalk, extort, blackmail, or encourage self-harm or suicide.
6. Spam, phishing, scams, pyramid schemes, or solicit money, services, or donations.
7. Use automation, bots, scraping, or attempt to reverse-engineer or tamper with the app.
8. Share copyrighted material you do not own or have rights to distribute.
9. Encourage or coordinate violence, terrorism, or other criminal activities.

Image, audio, and video rules
• Do not upload or request sexually explicit media of anyone who appears under 18.
• Pornographic content is disallowed in public or unmatched chats. If the app allows private exchanges, explicit sexual content still requires mutual, informed consent and must comply with local law.
• Misuse of face recognition or attempting to identify people from images is prohibited.

Privacy
• Never share your private login credentials.
• Protect your personal information; do not give out addresses, financial details, or government ID.
• If you receive threatening or abusive messages, use the in-app report function and block the user.

Data Responsibility
OmeDash is not responsible for information, messages, media, or personal details that users voluntarily share in open, public, or unmatched chats. Users share such information at their own risk. Do not post anything you would not want to be publicly accessible or retained in logs.

Reporting and moderation
• Use the in-app report button or contact support at omedash.help.com to report violations. Include screenshots, usernames, and timestamps when possible.
• Moderators review reports and may remove content, issue warnings, suspend, or permanently ban accounts.

Enforcement and penalties
Consequences depend on severity and may include:
• Warning.
• Temporary restriction (12 days) for moderate violations or a first serious offense.
• Permanent ban for severe or repeated violations.
• Referral to law enforcement when required by law (for threats, exploitation, child sexual abuse material, or other criminal behavior).
• Deactivation or deletion of accounts that attempt to evade enforcement.

Appeals
If you believe an enforcement action was made in error, submit an appeal to omedash.help.com with evidence and a clear explanation. Appeals will be reviewed; enforcement may remain in effect during review.

Content retention and law
Content, chat logs, and metadata may be retained for investigation and legal compliance. We cooperate with lawful authorities when required.

Changes to the rules
These rules may be updated. Significant changes will be posted in the app. Continued use after updates means you accept the new rules.

Quick do/don’t checklist
Do:
• Be respectful and obtain consent.
• Use the report and block features for problems.
• Keep personal data private.
Don’t:
• Share sexual content involving minors.
• Harass, threaten, or dox others.
• Scam, spam, or promote illegal activity.
